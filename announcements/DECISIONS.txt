openDAW announcement decisions and style
========================================
Notes to remember how the user wants announcements written. Keep this updated.

Writing style (all channels)
• No dashes (em or en dashes). Use commas or periods instead.
• No semicolons. Split into two sentences, or use a comma.
• Plain language. No marketing speak (for example, avoid "The point:").

Per channel
• Discord: supports markdown. Big headline uses "# " and starts with "Update: ", sublines use "### ".
  All headlines in Title Case (keep brand casing like openDAW and Nextcloud). Links use markdown
  masked links [label](url). Casual and factual, no emoji, no salesy phrasing.
• LinkedIn: professional. Hashtags at the end.
• Newsletter: focus on the schools angle. No greeting line (no "Hi ..."). No beta or pilot note.

Links
• openDAW: https://opendaw.studio
• Nextcloud manual: https://opendaw.studio/manuals/nextcloud

Feature framing (Nextcloud)
• Audience: schools and music classes first.
• Each student has their own Nextcloud account and own space, isolated from other students.
• Projects and assets stay on the school's own Nextcloud. openDAW talks to it directly. Nothing
  passes through openDAW servers.
• Samples and soundfonts are deduplicated, stored once and reused.
• openDAW is free and open.
• This is not a beta. Do not call it a beta.
